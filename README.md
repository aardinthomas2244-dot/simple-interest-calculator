# simple-interest-calculatAI Overview      
A simple interest calculator computes interest on loans or savings without compounding, using the formula: $SI = \frac{P \times R \times T}{100}$. It requires three inputs: Principal amount ($P$), interest rate ($R$), and time period ($T$). The total repayment includes principal plus interest ($A = P + SI$). [1, 2]

Simple Interest Formula Breakdown 

•  (Interest): Total interest amount. 
•  (Principal): Initial sum of money. 
•  (Rate): Annual interest rate (percent). 
•  (Time): Term in years (if in months, divide by 12; if in days, divide by 365). [1, 2, 3, 4, 8]  

How to Calculate (Example)To calculate $10,000$ at a $10\%$ rate for $6$ years: 

1. Formula: $10000 \times \frac{10}{100} \times 6 = 6000$. 
2. Result: The total interest is $6,000$. 
3. Total Amount: $10,000 + 6,000 = 16,000$. [4]
